How to Get to Borongan, Eastern Samar

Borongan is a coastal city located in the province of Eastern Samar in the Philippines. Here are some ways to get to Borongan:

By Air

The nearest airport to Borongan is Tacloban Airport (TAC) in Tacloban City, Leyte. From Tacloban, you can take a van or a bus to Borongan, which takes about 3-4 hours. There are several airlines that fly to Tacloban from major cities in the Philippines, such as Manila, Cebu, and Clark.

By Sea

You can also reach Borongan by sea through the ports of Allen, Northern Samar or Liloan, Southern Leyte. From there, you can take a bus or a van to Borongan. The trip may take longer than traveling by air, but it can be a scenic and enjoyable experience.

By Land

If you prefer to travel by land, you can take a bus from Manila or Cebu to Borongan. The trip may take several days, depending on the mode of transportation and the number of stops. However, it can be a great way to see the sights and experience the culture of the Philippines.

For more information about how to get to Borongan, you can contact the Borongan Tourism Office at +63 55 236 538 or visit their website at borongan.gov.ph.
